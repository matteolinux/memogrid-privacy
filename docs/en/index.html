Privacy Policy - MemoGrid

Last updated: November 26, 2025

Data Collection and Use

MemoGrid is designed with privacy as an absolute priority. The application:

  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not transmit information to external servers
  • Does not share data with third parties
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services

Data Storage

All data created in the app (mnemonic cards, patterns, passwords) is saved exclusively on the local device. Users have full control over their data and can delete it at any time by uninstalling the application.

Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, contact us through the App Store support page.
